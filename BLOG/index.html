One of the barangays in the municipality of La Castellana is Barangay Cabacungan. You can visit many tourist attractions in the mentioned barangay. Although it is only a little barangay, it is full with amazing places where you can enjoy the outdoors, water spring resorts, delectable cuisine, and the stunning view of Mount Kanlaon.

Mambangon/Caduhada Spring Resort

Mambangon/Caduhada Spring Resort is one of the stunning tourist spots in Barangay Cabacungan. It is located at Sitio Mambangon, Brgy. Cabacungan, La Castellana, Negros Occidental. Mambangon is known for it's clean, fresh, and crystal clear water. And even the tourists or travelers from distant or various locations travel for hours just to visit Caduhada Spring Resort or Mambangon, especially during summer or "tag-init". And the entrance fee is only Php 50.00, the cottage is also affordable. In Caduhada Spring Resort/ Mambangon, you can rest, chill, relax, and enjoy the nature.

Baji-Baji Falls/Busay

Baji-baji Falls is one of the best tourists spots here in Brgy. Cabacungan La Castellana Negros Occidental. It is known for it's beautiful scenery and instragamable spots. It is surrounded by mountain rocks and aquatic plants that made the falls look attractive and refreshing. It is also good for adventurous activities and it is near the Mystic forest.

Baji-Baji Spring Resort/Mystic Forest

Baji-Baji Spring Resort is also famous throughout Brgy. Cabacungan, it is known for it's fresh water and surrounded by huge trees which make the resort look elegant. It is located in purok 6. Local people in the barangay called this resort as baji-baji but the new owner did the renovation and now it is called the "Mystic Forest" in year 2020.

Cabacungan Public Plaza

Every Barangay has a public plaza, but the one in Brgy Cabacungan is special since it is the ideal location to take in the breathtaking front view of Mt. Kanlaon. Additionally, it is where travelers and riders halt to soak in the breathtaking ambiance and photograph the "I love Cabacungan" sign, which adds even more beauty to the public plaza. With its 100+ mahogany trees, lush grass, basketball court, volleyball and takraw nets, as well as comfortable seats for local Brgy residents, the public plaza is also brimming with color and excitement. Cabacungan. Pack your bags and get ready to experience beauty and a "OUT OF THIS WORLD" feeling.
